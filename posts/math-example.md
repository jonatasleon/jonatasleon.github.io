This post demonstrates how to use math equations in your blog posts.

## Inline Math

You can use inline math with single dollar signs: $E = mc^2$ or $P(A|B) = \frac{P(B|A) \cdot P(A)}{P(B)}$.

## Block Math

For block equations, use double dollar signs:

$$
P(A|B) = \frac{P(B|A) \cdot P(A)}{P(B)}
$$

Here's another example with a more complex equation:

$$
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} dx = \sqrt{\pi}
$$

## Matrix Example

You can also display matrices:

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
a & b \\
c & d
\end{bmatrix}
\begin{bmatrix}
x \\
y
\end{bmatrix}
=
\begin{bmatrix}
ax + by \\
cx + dy
\end{bmatrix}
$$

## Summation

$$
\sum_{i=1}^{n} i = \frac{n(n+1)}{2}
$$

Math equations are now fully supported in your blog posts!
